Menu of the Day

Chicken

Tandoori Chicken is a cooking method that has become synonymous with the food that is prepared. Marinated meat being cooked over an intense fire in a clay oven. The meat is lowered into the oven on long metal skewers and cooked in this smoky and extremely hot environment until done.

Beef

A steak pie is a traditional meat pie served in Britain. It is made from stewing steak and beef gravy, enclosed in a pastry shell. Sometimes mixed vegetables are included in the filling. The dish is often served with "steak chips" (thickly sliced potatoes fried, sometimes in beef dripping).

Fish

Fried sea bass fish or cod fish in batter served with large double-cooked chunky chips, lemon wedges and garden peas or mushy peasand a splash of home made vinaigrette. Give a try to our fresh new panko crusted cod, seaweed salted chips with homemade tartar sauce.
